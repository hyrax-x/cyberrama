About the Project:

After watching the anime series CYBERPUNK: Edgerunner, I instantly feel in love with the story and the world. Spent many hours in the game, I found a lot of similar location between the scenes in the show and in the game. The aim of this project is to somewhat compare the similarity of some famous scenes in the anime!
